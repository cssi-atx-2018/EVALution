History

We want to provide a non-intrusive resource that people who feel isolated will want to use. Designed to be a safe, secure place to express yourself, the Glass Bottle allows users to write about their feelings anonymously and then automatically deletes any text typed into its field. This is perfect for those struggling with depression who may not feel confident enough to confide in a real person. Our website then helps these individuals gain confidence and recuperate by offering various levels of sharing: users can first vent without saving, post anonymously to support others in their positions, and, in the future, join anonymous chats where they discuss their issues with real people who have struggled with the same issues.


How it Works

How does the Glass Bottle work? To provide a space to feel heard, the Glass Bottle acts as a way for you to express your feelings. For full anonymity, the text that you type isn’t even sent to our page. It exists on the surface of your computer and vanishes when you press “send”. Unless you choose so, your thoughts don't even leave your home or cross the internet. However, if you do feel like sharing your writing with people in similar situations, you can log in and press “post” to share your anonymous thoughts to positively impact others.
